Day Tab

A calm Chrome new-tab page: your Google Calendar day, a to-do list, a scratchpad, and the day's weather — on paper and bronze, with nothing fighting for your attention.

What Day Tab does

Day Tab is a free, open-source Chrome browser extension that replaces your browser's new-tab page with a personal daily dashboard. With your permission, it connects to your Google account to display your Google Calendar events (read-only — it can never create, change, or delete anything) and your list of calendars so you can choose which to show. Alongside the calendar it provides a to-do list, a scratchpad, and a weather summary. All data is displayed in your browser only: Day Tab has no servers, and your calendar information is never stored or sent anywhere. See the privacy policy for details.

Features

  • Day view of your Google Calendar (read-only), with countdown pills on events starting within the hour.
  • Week strip with event-density dots — click to jump.
  • Month overlay — press M for the full month.
  • Weather per morning / afternoon / evening via Open-Meteo (keyless, account-free).
  • To-dos and a scratchpad, synced across your Chrome profile.
